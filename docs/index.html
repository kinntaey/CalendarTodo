Plan Todo

Your calendar and to-dos, finally in one place.

About

Plan Todo brings your calendar and to-do lists together in one clean, intuitive app. Plan your week, manage daily tasks, and stay connected with friends.

Features

  • Month, week, and day calendar views
  • To-do lists with categories, priorities, and reminders
  • Apple Calendar sync
  • Share to-do lists and events with friends
  • Home screen widget

Privacy Policy

Plan Todo collects only the minimum data necessary to provide its services:

  • Account information — email address and display name for authentication
  • Calendar data — stored on your device and optionally synced with Apple Calendar
  • Profile photo — optional, stored securely

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. All data is transmitted securely via HTTPS. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings.
